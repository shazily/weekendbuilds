What is Vibe with Kids?

A open-source collection of games and experiments that a dad and his 9-year-old built together — vibe coding with AI as a learning tool, not a shortcut.

Who we are

I'm Shazily Munawar. My son Zizou (9) and I share a passion for games, building new things, and learning together. We started vibe coding on weekends — describing ideas out loud, iterating with AI, and shipping small playable projects.

Why we open-sourced this

We decided to share what we make so other parents and kids can try the same thing at home. Vibe with Kids is a fun way to learn together — talking through ideas, testing them quickly, and understanding how AI can be a creative partner rather than a black box.

  • Every project here was built with AI assistance — prompts, iteration, and debugging together.
  • Each game is a single HTML file you can download, open, and remix.
  • New builds drop on Weekend Labs as we finish them.
