Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Vibe with Kids (Weekend Builds) is an open-source collection of browser-based projects built by Shazily Munawar and his son Zizou. We respect your privacy.

What we collect

  • Camera/microphone: Some projects (Perfect Blue, Sound Wizard) request camera access locally in your browser to power hand-tracking and visual effects. Video is processed on your device and is not sent to our servers.
  • Ideas page: If you submit a prompt or like an idea, we store the text you provide and an anonymous like count. Submissions may be reviewed before appearing publicly.
  • Hosting analytics: Our site is hosted on Vercel, which may collect standard web server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited) as part of normal hosting operations.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell personal data.
  • We do not require accounts or collect children's personal information through forms.
  • We do not store camera feeds from your device on our servers.

Local storage

Some features (such as remembering which prompts you liked) use your browser's local storage on your device only.

Third-party services

Downloaded HTML files may load libraries from CDNs (e.g. MediaPipe, Tone.js, Three.js). Those services have their own privacy policies.
